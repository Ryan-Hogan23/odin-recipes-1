Chickpea Sandwich

Tasty, Quick, Healthy Sandwich

If you are looking for a delicious alternative to a tuna sandwich, look no further than this simple, yet tasty sandwich.

This sandwich ticks all the boxes, for vegetarians and meat eaters alike. It's a simple yet satisfying sandwich, that tickles the taste buds and is not to harsh on your pocket.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 3 Tbsp mayo (can always replace with a vegan substitute)
  • 1 Tbsp honey/maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of red onion
  • 2 Tbsp fresh dill (finely chopped)
  • Pinch of salt, pepper and paprika
  • Bread of your choosing
  • Spinach/Lettuce, avocado, tomato and cheese

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse chickpeas, add to bowl and lightly mash. Then add mayo, honey, red onion, dill, salt, pepper, paprika and mix with a spoon.
  2. Toast bread (if desired) and prepare the rest of the sandwich fillings (spinach, tomato, avocado).
  3. Scoop a healthy amount of filling onto two pieces of bread, add desired toppings, and top with another slice of bread.
