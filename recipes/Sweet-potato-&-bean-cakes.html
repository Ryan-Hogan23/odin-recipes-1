Sweet Potato and Bean cakes

A Diverse, Healthy, Fast dish

This recipe is another one of my favourites.

If you are looking for a meal with minimal effort but has maximum taste, look no further as this is the dish for you. It's great as a snack, perfect for burgers/sandwiches, also its great for luch prep as you can freeze a batch and thaw them as you need.

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato, cut into cubes
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 1 finely diced chilli
  • 1 Tbsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Cook sweet potato cubes at 220deg in your oven for 20mins (or until soft).s
  2. Lightly mash the beans in a bowl, add cooked potato, spring onion, seasoning and mash until mix is combined.
  3. Shape mix into 4 cakes and leave them in the fridge for 1hr.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, then fry the bean cakes for 4-5mins each side.
