Bean Quesadilla

Healthy, Fast, Cheap Bean Quesadilla

This recipe is simple, fast and a perfect dish to cook no matter the occasion.

It is perfect to cook for family get togethers, also perfect as a snack or great to amke a big batch for lunch prep. With very minimal ingredients and prep time it is sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 15oz can black beans
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup red onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 bunch fresh parsley
  • 2 cups shredded chesse
  • 1 batch taco seasoning
  • 10 flour tortillas
gi

Steps

  1. Drain the black beans and add them to a bowl along with frozen corn
  2. Finely dice the onion, garlic, and roughly chop the parsley
  3. Combine onion, garlic, parsley, shredded cheese, and taco seasoning to the bowl with beans and corn. Stir until everything is evenly combined and coated in seasoning.
  4. Place a half cup of filling on one side of each tortilla and fold over. Cook the quesadillas in a skillet over medium heat on each side until brown and crispy and the cheesy filling has melted. Slice into triangles then serve.
